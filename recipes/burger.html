Delicious Burger Recipe

Home

This classic burger recipe is perfect for a quick and tasty meal.
This recipie contains the secret to making the best burger known to human kind

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1 slice of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 equal patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or skillet for about 1 minute.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the buns and adding your favorite toppings and condiments.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
