Delicious Pizza Recipe

Home

This classic pizza recipe is perfect for a fun and tasty meal.
This recipe contains the secret to making the best pizza known to human kind

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Dried oregano and basil to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings evenly across the pizza.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the top and sprinkle with dried oregano and basil.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve!
